Executive Summary

During my 13-week data engineering internship at ACA Group, I contributed to the "Sustainathon 2025" project - a mission-critical initiative to revolutionize Duvel Moortgat's sustainability reporting for CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) compliance. This project transformed a functional proof-of-concept into a production-ready, maintainable platform that supports comprehensive regulatory requirements.

The implementation delivered substantial technical improvements through code optimization and architectural restructuring. I reduced the codebase from over 300 lines to under 80 lines using dynamic Jinja templating across 39 models, improved processing time from 45 minutes to 20 minutes, and implemented a simplified data model with enhanced error handling throughout the system.

Project Context

Duvel Moortgat NV, Belgium's premier brewery group with facilities across multiple countries, needed to comply with the European CSRD regulations. This legislation requires comprehensive sustainability reports covering environmental impact, social aspects, and governance metrics with precise documentation and audit trails.

Working alongside fellow intern Fabian Reyes under the mentorship of Kwinten Boes (Project Manager) and Thibo Vanderkam (Data Engineer), I focused on data engineering aspects while Fabian concentrated on dashboard development and client communication.
